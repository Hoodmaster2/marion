About Us

Hope & Victory Foundation is a non-profit humanitarian organization committed to promoting self-reliance, dignity, and sustainable community development.

Vision

A better future and right livelihood for families and individuals in our villages.

Mission

Improving the quality of life through sustainable local development initiatives in Kenya.

Our Programs & Activities

  • Community Development
  • ICT Training & Empowerment
  • HIV/AIDS, Malaria & TB Awareness
  • Support for Orphans & Vulnerable Children
  • Volunteer Deployment & Cultural Exchange
